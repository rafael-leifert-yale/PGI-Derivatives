## Key Takeaways

### What makes money
- **3-DTE is the magic number.** Every single profitable strategy uses 3-DTE entry.
- **Wings help.** Selling 20-wide wings boosted Sharpe from 7.04 to 13.07 and win rate from 42% to 69%.
- **Delta threshold barely matters for 3-DTE.** All thresholds from 1 to 15 were profitable with 3-DTE.
- **Hold to EOD.** P&L stops always made things worse (0% win rate).

### What loses money
- **0-DTE.** All 76 zero-DTE configurations lost money. Theta decay and bid-ask spreads are too punishing.
- **Wide strangles.** Strangle width > 10 on 0-DTE destroys win rate (drops to <10%).
- **P&L stops.** Stop losses always get triggered; take profits never do. They just lock in losses faster.
- **7-DTE.** Too much theta to overcome with gamma scalping alone.
- **No hedging at all (dH=100).** Gamma scalping without hedging is just buying straddles and praying.
- **2-DTE.** Despite having the highest win rate (44%), average losses on losing days swamp the wins.

### The DTE sweet spot explained
| DTE | Total P&L | Why |
|----:|----------:|-----|
| 0 | -$356 to -$1,744 | Bid-ask spread too wide relative to premium, theta crushes you in hours |
| 1 | -$1,193 | Still too much overnight theta, not enough gamma runway |
| 2 | -$2,074 to -$2,212 | Worst of both worlds: expensive options + trending market exposure |
| **3** | **+$540 to +$2,557** | **Sweet spot: enough gamma runway to capture moves, options still liquid** |
| 5 | -$1,238 | Too much time value, theta drag accumulates over 5 days |
| 7 | -$2,647 | Maximum theta exposure, too few trades for statistical relevance |

## Column Definitions

| Column | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **Total P&L** | Total profit/loss over the 3-month backtest period (1 contract per trade) |
| **Avg P&L** | Average profit/loss per trade |
| **Sharpe** | Annualized Sharpe ratio (risk-adjusted return) |
| **Win%** | Percentage of trades that were profitable |
| **PF** | Profit Factor = gross profits / gross losses (>1 = profitable) |
| **Trades** | Number of trades executed in the period |
| **Avg Hedges** | Average number of delta hedges per trade |
| **Avg Cost** | Average transaction costs per trade (commissions + fees + slippage) |
| **dH=N** | Delta hedge threshold: hedge when portfolio delta exceeds N shares |
| **DTE** | Days to expiration at entry |
| **EOD** | Exit at end of day |
